Crispy Orange Beef

Home

Description

A delicious orange beef recipe that is crispy and sweet yet mildly spiced. This beef stir-fry recipe is great served with steamed rice and broccoli.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds beef top sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup long grain rice
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/3 cup rice wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons frozen orange juice concentrate
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups oil for frying
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 3 tablespoons grated fresh ginger
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 2 teaspoons orange zest
  • 8 broccoli florets, lightly steamed or blanched

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Lay beef in a single layer on a baking sheet lined withpaper towels.
    Allow to dry in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, bring water and rice to a boil in a medium saucepan.
  4. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender, about 20 minutes.
  5. Mix together sugar, rice vinegar, orange juice concentrate, soy sauce,
    and salt in a small bowl. Set aside.
  6. Heat oil in a wok over medium-high heat. Toss dried beef in cornstarch to coat.
  7. Fry coated beef in hot oil in small batches until crispy and golden brown;
    set aside. Drain oil from the wok, leaving about 1 tablespoon.
  8. Add ginger, garlic, and orange zest to remaining oil in wok.
  9. Cook and stir briefly until fragrant. Pour in soy sauce mixture;
    bring to a boil and cook until thick and syrupy, about 5 minutes.
  10. Stir in fried beef and cook until heated through.
  11. Serve hot over steamed rice and garnish with broccoli.
